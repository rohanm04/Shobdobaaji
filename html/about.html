About

I'm Rohan Mallik, a writer who finds artistry in the overlooked moments of everyday life. I write in both Bengali and English, and my work spans short stories and poems. Though primarily rooted in fiction, much of my writing draws inspiration from real-life experiences and the human element of it. Every story, every character, is essentially a part of me; an extension of who I am.

This website is home to all my writings: poems, short stories, collections, verses, and everything in-between. I write with the hope of reaching readers who discover a piece of themselves in my words.

Outside of writing, you'll most likely catch me reading, watching films, or scribbling random ideas into a notebook. I have a habit of talking people's ears off, yet it's often the silences in-between that inspire me most.

Thank you for visiting and for taking the time to read my work. I hope you find something here that resonates with you.
