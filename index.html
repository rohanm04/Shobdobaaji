Latest Work:

Whispers of the River
Story
I picked up a smooth, flat stone from the riverbank and flicked it across the water. It skipped elegantly three times, bouncing through the pale white glisten of the sun's reflection before drowning in the river with a final splash. Turning around, I caught sight of Kannu, who had the same idea. His eyes scanned the bank for the flattest of stones. As our gazes met, he grinned and yelled out, “The one with the most skips wins!”...
